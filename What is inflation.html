What is inflation?

Simply, you can say that inflation is a rate on how a price on something increases over time. Further explanation and examples are shown below.

Example

Let's say 1kg of orange cost $1.00 in 2023. And the inflation rate is 5% which is 5 cents($0.05). So, the 1kg of orange in 2024 will cost $1.05. This is an explanation of how an inflation works. If you know how it works you may also have to know the impact of it. If you buy $20 of orange, you will get 20kg of oranges in 2023. After the time flies to 2024, you might buy $20 of oranges again, but this time you will only get about 19kg of oranges. That is the impact of it. The more the time goes, the more the prices increse. And you are getting less number of things as a result.
